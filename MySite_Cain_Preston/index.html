Super Mario 3D World

Save the sprixies

Big, bad Bowser and his crew have invaded the peaceful Sprixie Kingdom. Now it's up to Mario and his friends to rescue the Sprixies by fighting their way through a vast new kingdom. Play solo or with up to three friends in this new Mario platforming adventure!

Characters

Mario’s back—and he’s brought friends. Choose one of four characters and master their special skills as you run, jump, swim, and scamper your way through this wild adventure.

When Mario gets a call for help, he leaps into action. Help him make his way through perilous challenges and tons of baddies.

Luigi would never let his big brother Mario go it alone. Use Luigi’s super jumping skills to hurdle high platforms.

This time, the princess is the one doing the rescuing. Her special floating ability helps her across any obstacle that comes her way.

This little fella is ready for a big adventure. Use his extra-fast running speed to get out of sticky situations.

An adventure so big, it is only possible on Wii U

Play with up to three friends at once for multiplayer Mario magic! Players can jump in at any time to lend a hand or help find hidden stars—or compete for the most coins and win the crown at the end of each level.

Powerups

You've never seen Mario like this! Pick up a Super Bell to transform into a cat. Scamper up walls to find secrets, pounce from above, and attack enemies with your claws—while looking pawsitively adorable.

Boost your firepower big-time with the Double Cherry power-up, which creates a clone of your character. Keep collecting them to see how many doubles you can create!

Look for these (and lots more) special items as you play.

Concerts

Sterling Selections

November 6     7:30 p.m.
Hoag Recital Hall, CSU Pueblo

The season opens with a lighthearted concert featuring a variety of music, including the lilting strains of Brahms’ Liebleider Waltzes and his Zigeunerlieder (love songs and Gypsy songs).

Guest artists will be the Freemont Civic Choir, under the direction of Charles Rann.

Salvation

February 26     7:30 p.m.
Hoag Recital Hall, CSU Pueblo

The majesty of Handel’s Messiah unfolds as excerpts from the Easter portion are presented with orchestral accompaniment.  The CSU Pueblo Concert Choir and Pueblo West High School Choir join the Choral in performing this great Lenten masterpiece

Shimmering Silver Gala

April 30     7:30 p.m.
Pueblo Convention Center

Stolen silver sparks Victor Hugo’s classic French tale as Jean Valjean and his relentless pursuer Inspector Javert capture the spotlight at the Gala.

Enjoy an evening of dining, dancing, and broadway musical entertainment as the Chorale performs both lyrical and haunting melodies from Schonberg’s Les Miserables.
